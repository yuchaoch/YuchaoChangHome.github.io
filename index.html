Short Bio

He received the Ph.D. degree in Shanghai Institute of Microsystem and Information Technology from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, China, in June 2019. From January 2018 to January 2019, he joined Prof. Prof. Naofal Al-Dhahir (IEEE Fellow) Group in University of Texas at Dallas as a Visiting Ph.D. Student, USA. From July 2019 to January 2021, he was a senior Research Staff in Shanghai Huawei Technologies Company, Ltd., China. From February 2021 to October 2021, he is a Postdoctoral Fellow at Department of Electronic Engineering in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China. His research interests include green communications, co-optimization of performance and power for artificial intelligence, and machine learning applications.
