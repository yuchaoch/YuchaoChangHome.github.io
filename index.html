Short Bio

I received the Ph.D. degree in Shanghai Institute of Microsystem and Information Technology from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, China, in June 2019. From January 2018 to January 2019, I was a Visiting Ph.D. Student in University of Texas at Dallas, Texas, USA. From July 2019 to January 2021, I was a senior Research Staff in Shanghai Huawei Technologies Company, Ltd., China. From February 2021 to October 2021, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at Department of Electronic Engineering in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China. My research interests include green communications, green computing, AI/ML applications, and modeling and algorithm.
